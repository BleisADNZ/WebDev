Frequently Asked Questions

When will we be able to buy/mint some!?



July 3rd 2:00 PM EST - We will be monitoring gas and come up with a official time window in our LIVE AMA

Will we be able to buy them through your website or opensea?

Our developers have enabled minting/buying of elephants directly on our website through a ERC-721 Smart Contract! This enables us to have a smooth launch and not have to worry about our community having gas wars, or having any transaction fail when trying to mint.

What will the price be per mint?

0.06 ETH - No Bonding Curve(Money Grab) , We believe strongly that bonding curves are not fair distribution.

Who are we going to be donating to?

In conjunction with Save the Elephants, Wildlife Conservation Network, and The Leonardo DiCaprio Foundation, the Elephant Crisis Fund (ECF) was formed in 2013 as a 100% donation run model. What this means is that every dollar donated to the fund is used directly towards projects in place that protect elephants. ZERO dollars are used towards administrative fees or overhead!

How much do you guys plan on contributing?

The Untamed Elephants Crew looks to help get them towards their goal of 50 million dollars by 2023 by bringing awareness to the issues surrounding elephant preservation. Typically, the ECF funds projects between $15,000 to $150,000 and the Untamed Elephants Crew looks to sponsor a large venture, pledging to donate $100,000 once we reach 50% sales, with donations and participation of crew members to follow in the future.

Why did you pick this one out of 100s?

The project holds a special place in our hearts, as it does most animal lovers! By funding anti-poaching, anti-trafficking, demand reduction of ivory, and human-elephant coexistence around the world, ECF looks towards a better future for elephants and preserving those specific species that are endangered. Please visit their website for more information, and let us know if you are interested in teaming up with us to help the Elephant Crisis Fund! https://www.elephantcrisisfund.org/

Whats going on in your smart contract?

Our smart contract follows the OpenZeppelin Standards for ERC721 and ERC721-Enumerable, it is a simple NFT contract for our Untamed Elephants, we are also making sure that all our metadata and images of the Elephants are uploaded to IPFS to create NFTs that last forever on the blockchain, and the metadata is structured to comply with OpenSea standards.
